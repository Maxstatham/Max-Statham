The political value of the poor image is that the creation of the poor image sparked an abundance of accessible information. Secrets and opinions were no longer kept quiet and audiences became substantially larger. The more important value of these poor images is that as these images are downloaded, compressed, altered, appropriated, etc. in a continuous cycle, it touches the hands minds of a multitude of people. Through this exchange the poor image becomes a more united human reflection of own existence. It has the power to combine people and communities through mutual difference and agreement.
